Cleric Animation


This animation was my assessment submission for the animation module of my yoobee course

It's kinda ugly, but I'm honestly quite proud of it. I have never been known for my drawing skills, but this entire animation was hand drawn by me, in my 3rd (serious) attempt ever of drawing a person

The design is heavily based off of the nightsong from Baldurs Gate 3, a personal favourite game of mine.
The animation is designed to loop smoothly.
