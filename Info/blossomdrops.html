Blossomdrops


This is unarguably the worst photo on here, but I put it up anyway because it is my greatest dissapointment.

The image itself is of a bare winter tree with what looks like pink blossoms on it, however there are not flowers, but raindrops!

In person it was truly a sight to behold, as the raindrops were shining iridescently against the bare bark of the tree - so of course I tried to get a photo of it, but whatever I tried the photo always came out blurry and not able to truly capture the beauty of the light.
I am unsure if it was a limitation of my equipment, my knowledge/skill at the time, or if it was simply not possible to represent in a photo, but all the same it's a shame that I could not capture it.

So now I am left with a blurry photo of pink raindrops on a tree. Still beautiful in a way, but nothing compared to how it looked IRL.
