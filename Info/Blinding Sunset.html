Blinding Sunset


This image is a result of me trying to play with the rule of thirds.

Right down the middle we have the coastline, the across the middle we have the horizon, breaking the Ro3 in two different ways

But then, (hopefully) salvaging this, we have an eye catching ball of light on the left third, which contrasts really well I think with the rest of the image being so dark.

This photo was taken at tahunanui beach in nelson.
