Unnatural Colours


By now it's pretty obvious that I have a style. I really like light and the sky, and sunsets are something that combines those two perfectly. They may be basic, but in my opinion nothing is more beutiful than a well captured sunset.

I chose this image in paticular because of how soft it is. The haze in the air combined with the rosegold clouds and pale blue sky behind is breathtaking.

Because this wasn't a planned shot, it was taken in portrait rather than landscape, but honestly I think that adds to the charm!

There isn't much more to say about this one, it isn't amazing or a masterpiece, but it's soft and warm and I like it a lot.
