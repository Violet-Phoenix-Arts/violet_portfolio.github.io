Subtle crown


Once again, Light.

This time, a sunset. Or rather, the opposite horizon from the sunset.

This photo is supposed to represent Night chasing away the day, but supposed is in huge italics here, because unless you know where this photo was taken, it's not immediatly obvious that this is opposite from the sunset, it just looks like a normal sunset.
The name is based on the "Radiate crown", a symbol of the sun found across many cultures, representing the rays of the sun. Here, where the rays are put out vertically, it looksw even more crownlike.

And yet, these aren't rays coming from the sun, but rather faint lines on the opposite horizon. It is the crown of that which chases the sun away. (wow that sounds pretentious)

This photo was taken at sumner beach
